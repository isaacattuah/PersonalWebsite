About Me

Hello! I'm an AI/ML Customer Engineer at Google Cloud, based in Atlanta, GA. My passion lies at the intersection of cloud computing, artificial intelligence, and software development. I thrive on helping startups and digital natives leverage powerful technologies like Google Cloud's AI Platform (Vertex AI), BigQuery, and Looker to solve real-world problems and drive innovation.

With a background in Computer Science from the University of Miami (Cum Laude) and Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (First Class Honors), I bring a strong technical foundation. I enjoy tackling complex challenges, mentoring others, and continuously learning in this rapidly evolving field. My experience ranges from developing internal tools and dashboards to contributing significantly to customer success and deal value.

Key Areas: Google Cloud Platform (GCP), Artificial Intelligence / Machine Learning, Data Analytics (BigQuery, Looker), Cloud Architecture, Python, Terraform.
